Global Sea Ice

Climate change is one of the most significant threats to life and the environment. Extreme weather events have caused extensive damage to life and property all over the world. Researchers have since long used the rate of glacial melting to predict environmental hazards such as rising sea levels and hurricanes. Our goal for this project is to visualize data on sea ice and snow cover. We hope that visualizing this data will help convey the impact of climate change in a way that is easy to understand by researchers as well as the general public.
